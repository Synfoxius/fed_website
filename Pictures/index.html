About Me

Why Polytechnic Education and Not Junior College

I felt that after 10 years of the same style of education, I decided to try the Polytechnic Education System as I heard it was different from the Primary and Secondary School Education System put in place by the Ministry of Education. I am also a very consistent person, and Secondary School does not reward people with consistency as well as Polytechnic so my heart was set on the Poly Education Landscape. As for why I chose IT, I have a strong interest in the Technology Landscape and I have been reading up about Tech News since Secondary Two and I have built my own computer as well.
