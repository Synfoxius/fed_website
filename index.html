About Me

My name is Boris and I'm from Singapore Polytechnic Diploma of Information Technology, class DIT 1A/03. My username online is Synfoxius, if you recognise me then you must play the same video games as I do! I am a 17 year old male this year, just completed Secondary School Education and here I am in doing my Diploma.

Why Polytechnic Education and Not Junior College

I felt that after 10 years of the same style of education, I decided to try the Polytechnic Education System as I heard it was different from the Primary and Secondary School Education System put in place by the Ministry of Education. I am also a very consistent person, and Secondary School does not reward people with consistency as well as Polytechnic so my heart was set on the Poly Education Landscape. As for why I chose IT, I have a strong interest in the Technology Landscape and I have been reading up about Tech News since Secondary Two and I have built my own computer as well.

What do I do in My Free Time?

Well I play a lot of video games, I guess I'm what normal people call a gamer. I also like to watch Anime and I like to read up about technology. When people are in need of assistance in terms on what laptop they should purchase or whether they should have a custom build gaming computer, I'm always lending a helping hand to my fellow friends.
