Gaming.

The Heart and Soul of a Gamer.

Here are my top 3 favourite games!

Grand Theft Auto V/Online

One of my all time favourite series, Grand Theft Auto is an open world crime simulator game developed by Rockstar North and published by Rockstar Games. What I like about the game is that it has a rich story and the gameplay is extremely polished. Rockstar Games does not make this series yearly which means they take the time to ensure that every game they release is of top quality. I play Grand Theft Auto Online every now and then so if you play it too we can play together and have fun in the game!

Overwatch

The video game that I'm hooked onto at this stage is Overwatch, a First Person Shooter game developed by Blizzard Games and published by Blizzard Entertainment. It is a unique take on the genre as there are characters with high and low skill curves to suit everyone's style of playing. I mainly play the Support class and the heroes that I main are Mercy, Moira and Baptiste. I only recently started so I am not that good at the game, but nevertheless it is still fun and relaxing to play with my friends after a busy week!

FIFA

FIFA is a game that I have been playing since a really young boy partially due to the fact that my father is a football fan. We support Manchester United in real life and we occassionally duel each other in co-op mode. I have always felt that football's manager mode has a nice touch to it and I always challenge myself to go with a poor team and let it rise to glory with a constrained budget and poor players. I sadly do not play FIFA Ultimate Team but we can play co-op together someday!
